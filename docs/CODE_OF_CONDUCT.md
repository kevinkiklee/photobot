# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior includes:**

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal attacks
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.
