Solving the Trade-offs

Ensuring clear priorities and visibility for admins.

Priority Rules (Conflict Resolution)

1. The "Allow Wins" Rule

If a user has any role that is explicitly allowed for a feature, they gain access, even if they have another role that is disallowed.

✓ Reason: Prevents locking out active contributors who might have "Legacy" or "Newbie" roles still attached.

2. Specificity Ladder

  1. Channel Override: Highest priority (Force-disable in #announcements).
  2. Role Allowance: Next (User must have an allowed role).
  3. Server Default: Lowest (The "is it on for the server?" check).
